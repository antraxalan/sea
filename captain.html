CAPTAIN CASEY SCOTT
CAPTAIN CASEY SCOTT IS A 3RD GENERATION SPORTFISH CAPTAIN WITH OVER 25 YEARS EXPERIENCE.

He has participated in a number of Bill Fish & other Tournaments with wins throughout the East Coast, The Bahamas, Virginia Beach and the Outer Banks.
